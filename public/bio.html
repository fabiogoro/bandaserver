Bios

Ariane Stolfi

Architect, composer, programmer and musician, transits between languages and disciplines. Doctorate candidate on Sonology (ECA-USP), researching interactive interfaces on web technologies, visiting researcher at Queen Mary University of London at the Centre for Digital Music, makes installations and performances such ”Hexagrama essa é pra tocar”and ”Cromocinética”. Has joined festivals such Submidialogias, #DisExperimental, Virada Cultural, Bigorna, Dissonantes e Música?, SHA, Tecnoshamanism, and conferences such Web Audio Conference and Audio Mostly, maintains finetanks.com experimental net label and collaborates with Sonora feminist collective.

Fábio Goródscy

Programmer and tech enthusiast that loves music. Studying computers and music as a Computer Science Master Degree student at Institute of Mathematics and Statistics from University of São Paulo. Joined conferences that unites technology and music such as Sonologia 2016 and CMMR, both in São Paulo. Attended CELFI’s workshop about Mathematics and Computers Applications for Music in Buenos Aires. Frequently attends Computer Music study group in Institute of Mathematics and Statistics and Sound Studies group in School of Communication and Arts both from University of São Paulo.
